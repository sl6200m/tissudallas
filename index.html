About Tissue Research Dallas

Founded in 2010, Tissue Research Dallas has grown to become a leading center for advanced tissue analysis in the Southwest. Our facility is equipped with cutting-edge technology and staffed by board-certified pathologists and experienced laboratory professionals.

We are committed to advancing medical knowledge through rigorous research while providing clinicians with the most accurate and comprehensive tissue analysis services available.
